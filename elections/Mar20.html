March 2020 Elections

The March 2020 LME2 Elections were held on the 1st of March 2020. They featured Senate (1st class), and House of Representatives elections.


Incumbents are represented with the letters: Ꞧ for Representatives, Ꞩ for Senator, Ȼ for member of the presidential Cabinet.
