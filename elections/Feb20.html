February 2020 Elections

The February 2020 LME2 Elections were held on the 1st of February 2020. They featured Presidential, Vice-presidential, Senatorial (3nd class), and House of Representatives elections.


Incumbents are represented with the letters: Ꞧ for Representatives, Ꞩ for Senator, Ȼ for member of the presidential Cabinet, and Ꝟ for Vice-president.
