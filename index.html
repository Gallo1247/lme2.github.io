Let's Mock Elections

Let's Mock Elections 2 is a mock elections community, founded on the declining Let's Talk Elections community. It was lanched on the 24th of November, 2019.

February 2020 elections

The February 2020 LME2 Elections were held in February 2020. Jhon Pete was elected president, and Trees Warmer was elected vice-president. The Democratic Party also became the largest party in both houses of Congress.
